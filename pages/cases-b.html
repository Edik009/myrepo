Real-world response stories highlighting measurable risk reduction and operational continuity. Our work is defined by measurable outcomes that protect revenue, ensure compliance, and maintain trust. MedCore Devices

Industry: Medical Devices

Outcome: Protected $2B in high-value IP and safeguarded clinical operations.

Value: Preserved revenue continuity and reduced regulatory exposure.

The threat was not merely technical but existential—targeting the core value of the company. Our response combined tactical containment with strategic regulatory navigation. By demonstrating the exact path of data exfiltration to both technical and executive teams, we transformed the incident from a security problem into a unified business recovery effort. The 36-hour resolution became a benchmark for their operational resilience, leading to an ongoing board-mandated threat simulation program.

NovaPay Holdings

Industry: FinTech

Outcome: Secured customer assets and reduced fraud attempts by 72%.

Value: Improved trust metrics and customer retention.

The rising fraud rate was eroding the foundational trust of a FinTech platform. Our solution went beyond blocking attacks; it involved creating a controlled demonstration of the fraud ecosystem’s scalability for leadership. This tangible evidence secured immediate investment in new controls. The 72% reduction directly correlated with a measurable uptick in customer deposit activity, proving that security investment directly enables growth.

Helios Public Sector Network

Industry: Government

Outcome: Protected critical assets with a 45% faster containment rate.

Value: Strengthened resilience scoring and executive confidence.

Modernizing critical infrastructure under active threat requires precision. We employed a \"validate then migrate\" approach, using controlled simulations to prove vulnerabilities in isolated legacy segments before each migration phase. This de-risked the project politically and technically. The 45% improvement in containment speed is now a key performance indicator for their annual security governance review.
