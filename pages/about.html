Company Profile

Aegis Sentinel Global Cybersecurity Solutions

Founded in 2012 in Zug, Switzerland, Aegis Sentinel delivers elite cyber defense for regulated industries and enterprises where security failure is not an option. We operate where the stakes are highest—finance, healthcare, critical infrastructure, and government sectors—transforming cyber risk into a quantifiable and manageable strategic advantage.

Our mission is built on a foundation of anticipatory intelligence, not just reactive solutions. We combine deep threat research with hands-on adversarial simulation to expose vulnerabilities before they can be exploited. With over 280 specialists across global hubs in Zurich, London, Dubai, Singapore, Berlin, and Boston, our team includes former ethical hackers, intelligence analysts, and incident responders who understand both the attacker’s mindset and the defender’s imperative.

We don’t just protect systems; we strengthen organizational resilience through continuous validation, board-ready reporting, and compliance alignment with ISO 27001, 27701, and 22301 standards. As a Microsoft Intelligent Security Association partner, we integrate cutting-edge technology with human expertise to deliver outcomes—not just alerts.

At Aegis Sentinel, vigilance moves beyond the code. It’s embedded in our culture, our methodologies, and our unwavering commitment to defending the digital frontiers of our clients. We enable organizations to operate with confidence in a contested digital world.
